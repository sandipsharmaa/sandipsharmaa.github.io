Location + Mechanix = Locanix

Locanix is a big brand in commercial vehicle and logistics tracking devices. We met the owner at one of the conferences and exchanged the numbers in the networking session after a few days we received a call from him asking us to meet him since he had some work for us. We were very excited, it being such a big brand and went ahead to meet him.

He already had the website for the owners of logistics who buy these devices from Locanix and plug them with their vehicles to track them with certain details. He wanted to build the app for the same. One of the things that we realised after he, giving us some references of his competitors and how he wanted it to look different and what are the things that he wanted to keep.

After doing our R&D we came up with few solutions like changing the times the system wanted to ping to save on costs also how a contemporary look on app and some quick pointers which the user would require will add the ease of usability will affect the app as well as the website.

With this we gave the first prototype and gave a proposal to redesign the website if he wants us to so it can also match the website. He agreed with some changes. We went ahead to build this and after implementing the work the website and app are being used extensively not only our client but his clients are happy and the ease of usage for the end user has done the trick for him.
