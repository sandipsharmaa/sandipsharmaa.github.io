ABOUT ME

Hey, My name is Sandeep Sharma. I am a web and mobile interface designer from Ahmedabad, India.

I have been designing interfaces after my high school compilation in 2010 and since then I have evolved myself in the making of aesthetic interfaces, websites and mobile applications. From 2010 to 2013 I have worked with amazing people of Nichetech Computer Solutions Pvt. ltd. an Ahmedabad based IT Company and after that from 2013 I have turned myself into a full-time freelancer.

I like to design user-friendly Mobile Applications, Pixel-perfect Interfaces, Beautiful looking responsive websites. In my mean time I do Dribbbling, Instagraming and Tweeting. Also my biggest goal in life is to settle down in New York City (Dream City)
